About StartUpStock

The future success of any small company or start-up consists of its first investors that dare to take the risk by offering financial resources. Unfortunately, in our country there is currently no way for potential investors to track the financial status of a start-up so as to decide whether to invest in it or not.

Solution

StartUpStock platform helps people interested in investment and stock purchase, providing essential details on the financial status of start-ups or small companies. Each company will present their financial developments over a recent period of time, if they make profit or just cover their expenses, and also if they need other kind of resources, not necessarily money.


Through our platform, investors will have an overview of the financial status of a company and can choose more easily where to invest their money. Moreover, all the investors can leave feedback to any company or help with various tips, which is an opportunity for companies to evolve in the right direction.
